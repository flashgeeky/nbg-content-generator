**FAQ Input:**
```
Q: What is a red dot sight?
A: A red dot sight is a non-magnifying electronic optic that projects an illuminated red dot onto a lens, providing fast target acquisition with both eyes open.

Q: What MOA dot size should I choose?
A: 2 MOA is ideal for precision at distance, 4-6 MOA for faster acquisition in close quarters. Larger dots are easier to find but cover more of the target at range.

Q: Do red dots work in bright sunlight?
A: Quality red dots have brightness settings up to 12+ levels. Higher-end models include automatic brightness adjustment or solar panels for optimal visibility in any light.

Q: What's the difference between open and enclosed red dots?
A: Open reflex sights are lighter with a larger window, while enclosed sights offer better protection from debris and weather. Both are parallax-free and equally accurate.
```

## Tips for Best Results

### 1. Be Specific with Keywords
Instead of: `optics`
Use: `red dot sights, holographic sights, magnified optics`

### 2. Provide Context in FAQ
Generic: "What's the best optic?"
Better: "What's the best red dot sight for competitive 3-gun shooting?"

### 3. Use Natural Language
The generator excels at natural keyword integration, so don't worry about exact-match phrases

### 4. Leverage Refine Instructions
Use this field to:
- Adjust technical depth
- Target specific audiences
- Emphasize certain angles
- Request specific comparisons

### 5. Optimal Word Counts by Type
- **Brand Pages:** 750-1000 words
- **Category Pages:** 1000-1500 words
- **Product Pages:** 500-750 words (if implementing)

### 6. FAQ Best Practices
- 3-5 questions per page
- Address real buyer concerns
- Mix basic and advanced questions
- Keep answers detailed but scannable

### 7. Test and Refine
- Generate content
- Review for accuracy
- Use "Regenerate" if needed
- Add refine instructions to adjust tone
- Copy and paste into your CMS

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

### ❌ Keyword Stuffing
**Bad:** "red dot sight red dot sight best red dot sight"
**Good:** Natural mentions throughout educational content

### ❌ Too Vague
**Bad:** "guns, ammo, stuff"
**Good:** Specific product types and relevant terms

### ❌ Ignoring FAQ
FAQ sections are crucial for SEO and user experience - always include them

### ❌ Wrong Content Type
Use Brand pages for manufacturers, Category pages for product types

### ❌ No Refine Instructions
If first generation isn't quite right, use refine instructions rather than starting over

## Integration Workflow

1. **Research Phase**
   - Identify target keyword
   - List product variations
   - Gather related keywords
   - Collect common questions

2. **Generation Phase**
   - Fill out form completely
   - Generate content
   - Review output
   - Refine if needed

3. **Implementation Phase**
   - Copy main content to CMS
   - Add meta title and description
   - Insert FAQ schema markup
   - Add internal links
   - Add images (not generated by tool)

4. **Optimization Phase**
   - Monitor performance
   - Update based on data
   - Regenerate seasonally
   - Keep content fresh
